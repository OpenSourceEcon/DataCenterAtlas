# Texas data
To calculate Texas average effective property tax rates by county. we gather the following datasets for 2025 from the Texas Comptroller's website property taxes "[Tax Rates and Levies]((https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/property-tax/rates/index.php))" page (https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/property-tax/rates/index.php).

- 2025 School District Rates and Levies ([`2025-school-district-rates-levies.xlsx`](https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/property-tax/docs/2025-school-district-rates-levies.xlsx)) ([Texas Comptroller site](https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/property-tax/docs/2025-school-district-rates-levies.xlsx); DataCenterAtlas.org [GitHub repository]())
- 2025 City Rates and Levies (`2025-city-rates-levies.xlsx`) (Texas Comptroller site; Our GitHub repository)
- 2025 County Rates and Levies (`2025-city-rates-levies.xlsx`) (Texas Comptroller site; Our GitHub repository)
- 2025 Special District Rates and Leview (`2025-special-district-rates-levies.xlsx`)(Texas Comptroller site; Our GitHub repository)
